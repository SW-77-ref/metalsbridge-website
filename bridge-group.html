Bridge Group

    developing and operating the IT resources on which MetalsBridge runs

  • • BridgeTrade, LLC
    a 50/50 joint venture with Nippon Steel Trading Americas, Inc. that offers credit-insurable clients financing solutions in combined with MetalsBridge’s digital offering

  • • Bridge Consulting, LLC
    offering consulting services and advanced IT systems such as business intelligence (BI) and artificial intelligence (AI) designed to further enhance our clients online and offline supply chain management capabilities including

  • • Bridge Shipping & Chartering, LLC
    providing shipping and chartering solutions for domestic and international freight requirements for the metals and manufacturing industries

  • • mb Asset Recovery, LLC

  • • mbX, LLC
